Superset & Dropset Focus

(Chest, Shoulders, Triceps) - Focus on minimal rest between exercises in a superset (SS).

  • 1. Chest: Smith Machine Bench Press - 3 Sets of 10-12 Reps
  • 2. SS: Shoulders: Machine Shoulder Press - 3 Sets of 10 Reps
  • 2. SS: Shoulders: Dumbbell Lateral Raises - 3 Sets of 15 Reps
  • 3. Chest: Cable Crossover (Low to High) - 3 Sets of 12-15 Reps (Hold the squeeze)
  • 4. DS: Triceps: Rope Pushdown - 3 Total Sets (Each set is 12 reps, drop weight, 10 reps, drop weight, 8 reps)
  • 5. Shoulders: Single-Arm Dumbbell Upright Row - 3 Sets of 10-12 Reps (Per arm)
  • 6. SS: Triceps: Overhead Cable Extension - 3 Sets of 15 Reps
  • 6. SS: Chest: Push-ups - 3 Sets to Failure (Finisher)
