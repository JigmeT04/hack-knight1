Hypertrophy Volume Push

(Chest, Shoulders, Triceps) - Target 4 Sets per exercise.

  • 1. Chest: Machine Incline Press - 4 Sets of 10-15 Reps (Controlled eccentric)
  • 2. Shoulders: Dumbbell Shoulder Press - 4 Sets of 8-12 Reps
  • 3. Triceps: Skull Crushers (EZ Bar) - 4 Sets of 10-15 Reps (Focus on stretch)
  • 4. Chest: Pec Deck Fly - 4 Sets of 15-20 Reps (Partial reps after failure)
  • 5. Shoulders: Cable Lateral Raises - 5 Sets of 15 Reps (Short rest periods)
  • 6. Triceps: Rope Overhead Extension - 4 Sets of 12-15 Reps
  • 7. Shoulders: Reverse Pec Deck (Rear Delt) - 3 Sets of 20 Reps (Finisher)
