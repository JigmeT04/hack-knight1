Strength Focus (Heavy Lifts)

(Chest, Shoulders, Triceps) - Target 3-5 Sets per exercise.

  • 1. Chest: Low Incline Barbell Press - 4 Sets of 5-8 Reps (Heavy compound lift)
  • 2. Shoulders: Seated Military Press (Barbell) - 4 Sets of 6-10 Reps
  • 3. Chest: Machine Chest Press - 3 Sets of 8-12 Reps (Controlled tempo)
  • 4. Shoulders: Face Pulls (High/Rear Delt Warm-up) - 3 Sets of 15 Reps
  • 5. Triceps: Close-Grip Bench Press - 3 Sets of 8-12 Reps (Elbows tucked)
  • 6. Shoulders: Dumbbell Lateral Raises - 3 Sets of 10-15 Reps (Superset with Front Raises)
  • 7. Triceps: Dip Machine or Parallel Bar Dips - 3 Sets to Failure
